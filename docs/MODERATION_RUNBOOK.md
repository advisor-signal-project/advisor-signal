# Moderation runbook

1. Confirm the administrator is using a non-anonymous account present in `admin_roles`.
2. Read the relationship, time period, text, and structured fields; do not infer reviewer identity.
3. Reject doxxing, harassment, unsupported serious allegations, impersonation, spam, revenge content, or experience without a relevant relationship.
4. Approve only content focused on observable firsthand experience. Approval is policy acceptance, not factual certification.
5. Use `hidden` for previously approved content requiring removal; use `appealed` while reconsidering a decision.
6. Keep private notes minimal, factual, and free of unnecessary personal data. Every status action creates an audit event.
7. Review reports and correction requests promptly. Never disclose reviewer identity to a complainant.
8. Export moderation records only to an access-controlled location; never commit exports.
