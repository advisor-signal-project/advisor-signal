# Content policy

Allowed content describes firsthand outreach, interviews, offers, funding communication, meetings, mentorship, authorship expectations, workload, graduation expectations, lab culture, and career support with an approximate time period.

Disallowed content includes threats, harassment, slurs, hate speech, doxxing, student names, private contacts/addresses, passport or visa information, medical diagnosis, speculation about mental illness, unsupported criminal or sexual allegations stated as fact, fabricated quotations, identifying private screenshots, revenge submissions, impersonation, spam, manipulation, and pure insults.

Profiles, corrections, and institutional responses must use verifiable public sources. The system must never seed reviews about real people or import rumors.
